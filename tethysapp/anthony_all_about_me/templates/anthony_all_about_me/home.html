Anthony: All About Me

Birthplace: Arequipa, Arequipa, Peru

Arequipa is such a great city. I think some of the best memories of my childhood happened here. I lived here until I was 8 years old. I remember always watching the Misti Volcano from my bedroom window and wishing to be closer. Hiking up there is still on my bucket list.

This is the house where I was born

Food in Arequipa is amazing. In the following image we can see from left to right: ocopa arequipena, rocoto relleno, chupe de camarones and adobo arequipeno.

High School: Platteville Wisconsin

This is where I went to high school.

Mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

I was called to serve in the India, New Delhi Mission. I initially only had a one year visa to India, so when it expired I spent six months in the Philippines getting it renewed.




Family

My beautiful wife's name is Rachael and our cute little son Eli is 7 months old at this point.
